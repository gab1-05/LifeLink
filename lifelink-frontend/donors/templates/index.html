Why LifeLink

Built for emergencies.
Designed for speed.

When minutes matter most, LifeLink's real-time infrastructure connects the right donor to the right patient — instantly.

📍
Real-Time GPS Matching
Haversine-formula geo-search finds compatible donors within your chosen radius — sorted by distance and blood type.
⚡
Instant Notifications
Nearby donors get push alerts the moment an urgent request is posted. WebSocket-powered, zero delay.
💬
Live Chat
Direct, secure messaging between patients and donors so logistics can be coordinated immediately.
🗺️
Interactive Map
See available donors and open requests on a live map. Filter by blood type, distance, and urgency.
🏥
Hospital Integration
Hospitals can post requests directly for patients. Verified institution badges for trust.
📊
Donation Tracking
Donors track their history, eligibility dates, ratings, and lifetime impact in a personal dashboard.
Process

From request to donation
in under 5 minutes.

1
Post Request
Patient or hospital posts an urgent blood request with type, urgency, and location.
2
Match Donors
Compatible donors within 50 km are instantly notified via push and SMS alerts.
3
Chat & Confirm
Donor responds and coordinates with the patient through in-app live chat.
4
Save a Life
Donation happens, request marked fulfilled. Donor earns badges and ratings.
